An all-inclusive luxurious training week on the beautiful island of Zakynthos in Greece!

20-27 September 2025 - 8 participants only


Welcome to an all inclusive luxury freediving week on the beautiful Greek island of Zakynthos!

Two instructors, myself and Corinna Woerdemann, will take care of all your needs and make sure you have the best week of freediving!

You will be staying in a luxurious stone villa with a private pool (bedrooms are shared).

We have arranged for a private chef with extended experience in many restaurants throughout Europe to cook you lunch with local ingredients every day.

Breakfast is included, as well as dinner at a beautiful seaside restaurant where you will taste local delicacies cooked in a gourmet style.

We will be taking you to different dive sites and helping you explore this unique island that is one of the most popular in Greece.

On our day off diving we will have a private tour on a sailboat to the world famous Shipwreck Beach.

Freediving will be done from shore and the depth limit is -60m. You need to bring trainers and be able to hike a little bit to get to the water.

This week is open to all levels and we will be providing training and feedback tailored to your experience and needs.

Both Corinna and myself are also equalization instructors, so we will be taking extra time to help you become better and more aware with your equalization.

The total price per person is 1600 euros.

Here’s what’s included:

  • Accommodation in shared rooms
  • Transportation throughout the week
  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner
  • Sailboat tour
  • Freediving
  • Specialized equalization training both dry and in water as well as subscription to an online equalization course to prepare for the week
  • Weights
What’s not included:
  • Flights to/from the island of Zakynthos
  • Drinks (even during included meals) & snacks
  • Freediving equipment
A non-refundable deposit of 300 euros is required upon declaration of interest.

The remainder must be paid by the end of June and will be non refundable after that, unless you can find a replacement for your spot.

No refunds are to be issued if the weather is bad and diving is not possible for safety reasons.
