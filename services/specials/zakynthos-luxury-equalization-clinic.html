An all-inclusive luxurious equalization training week on the beautiful island of Zakynthos in Greece!

30 August - 5 September & 6 - 12 September 2026 - 8 participants only


Welcome to this all inclusive luxury freediving week on the beautiful Greek island of Zakynthos!

Two Apnea Academy instructors, myself and Corinna Woerdemann, will take care of all your needs and make sure you have the best week of freediving!

I am an equalization instructor with SHARE Equalization as well as Apnea Academy & Corinna is an equalization instructor with Apnea Academy. So, the main focus of the week will be getting to grips with equalization, with dry, pool and depth exercises. The students will all also be enrolled to an online equalization course to prepare for the clinic and to have a common language when we train in person.

You will be staying in a luxurious villa with a private pool (bedrooms are shared).

We will be having lunch at a traditional taverna where the (local but with international experience) chef uses local ingredients to prepare traditional recipes with a sophisticated touch.

Breakfast is included, as well as dinner at a beautiful seaside restaurant where you will taste local delicacies cooked in a gourmet style.

We will be taking you to different dive sites and helping you explore this unique island that is one of the most popular in the world.

On our day off diving we will have a private tour on a sailboat to the world famous Shipwreck Beach.

Freediving will be done from shore and the depth limit is -60m. You need to bring trainers and be able to hike a little bit to get to the water.

This week is open to all levels and we will be providing training and feedback tailored to your experience and needs.
