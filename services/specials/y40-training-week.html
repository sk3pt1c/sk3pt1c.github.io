A training week at the Y-40 deep thermal pool in the north of Italy!


Join me for a week of diving at the 42m deep Y-40 pool near Padova, Italy.

Only 3 students per session!

Y-40 is a thermal water pool situated in Montegrotto Terme, near Padova in the north of Italy.
The depth is 42m and the water temperature is around 34C, which means you don't need to wear a wetsuit to dive here!
Diving will be on weekdays, so you are to arrive on Sunday, dive Monday through Friday and depart on Saturday.
During the week, we will also explore the beautiful Italian countryside and numerous famous cities & sights like Venice, Verona etc.
We are going to be staying at a stunning villa, sharing the accommodation and having meals together in the large communal kitchen.
Every day after diving, we will be reviewing the videos of your dives and your overall experience to find things to work on for the next session.
I am an equalization instructor with Share and Apnea Academy so work on equalization awareness and control will be the key element during our time together, as well as corrections on technique etc of course.

Here is what's included:

  • Accommodation at Villa Petrarca (private room for each student)
  • Access to the Y-40 pool
  • Fins (included in the pool access, you need your ID to rent them for each session)
  • Training - 1 session per day
  • Daily videos of your diving shared in full quality royalty free
  • Car rental (we will need two cars to move around the area)
  • Transfers between the Montegrotto train station (about an hour from Venice airport) and our villa
  • Dive insurance
Not included / required:
  • Flights & Transportation to Montegrotto Terme
  • Food (we share food costs as we'll be eating together)
  • Towel for the pool
  • A mask & snorkel
  • A dive computer (you can rent one from the pool if you don't have one)
  • A small lock for your locker in the changing rooms of the pool

The total cost per person is 990 euros.

A non-refundable deposit of 200 euros is required to be part of the list and to be added to the relevant WhatsApp group.

The remaining 790 euros must be paid up to 3 months before arrival, after which deadline it will be considered non-refundable unless a replacement diver can be found.

Payment can be made via PayPal or Revolut.

For more information, please contact me directly using the links on the sidebar.
