A freediving week full of fun and activities on the island of Zakynthos in Greece!


1 October - 8 October

Only 4 students for the week!

I and my freediving friends will be your guides here on the island of Zakynthos and the nearby island of Kefalonia.

Here is what's included:

  • Accommodation at sea front rooms owned by a freediver/spearfisher friend (2 pax per room shared)
    - for a private room the total price for the week is 1200 euros
    (link to accommodation)
  • Dive insurance
  • A sailboat cruise to the Shipwreck Beach (roughly 8 hours on our off day)
  • A Private (spearfishing) tour to the nearby island of Kefalonia (the driver is a good personal friend, a freediver and spearfisher and is from Kefalonia originally so he knows the island's ins and outs).
  • One dive day using a boat and a counterballast system
  • Freediving training on the line
  • Cave exploration & fun diving
  • A big BBQ on your last night
Not included: gear (although I will give you weights so you don't need to bring those), your flights to and from the island, transportation on the island (I recommend renting a car) & food.

The cost per person is 950 euros.

Payment can be made via PayPal or Revolut.

For more information, please contact me directly using the links on the sidebar.
