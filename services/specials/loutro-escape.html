A unique swimming & freediving experience in a remote village only accessible by sea.


April 20-27 2025

Max 12 students, 3 per instructor per session

Imagine a magical village on Crete’s southwest coast, so secluded it feels like a hidden treasure.
This is Loutro — a tranquil haven accessible only by sea, where time slows down, and the modern world fades away.

Nestled in a protected bay, Loutro’s whitewashed houses line the shore, their reflections shimmering in turquoise waters as the Lefka Ori mountains rise dramatically above. The sea here is dazzlingly clear, with shades of blue so vivid they seem unreal, offering unparalleled visibility into its depths.

Your days in Loutro will unfold in a rhythm of relaxation and adventure. Mornings begin with gentle yoga and breathwork, preparing body and mind for a dive into the crystal-clear waters. A short swim leads to our freediving spot, a serene site sheltered from wind, with depths of 50 meters to explore under my and Corinna’s supervision and instruction.

During our dive sessions we will have a ratio of only 3 students per instructor.

Afternoons bring leisurely lunches at seaside tavernas, followed by a Greek siesta. Later, a traditional kaiki boat will take us along the untouched coastline for open-water swims (each person can swim at their own comfortable pace), exploring sea caves and vibrant underwater vistas with Noa’s expert supervision.

Evenings are for shared meals and planning the next day’s adventures. With hiking, freediving, and swimming, Loutro offers an unforgettable blend of nature and serenity.

You will have alternate swim and dive days to make sure you are not too tired and can enjoy the area in relaxation and comfort.

Join us for this one-of-a-kind escape and secure your spot ASAP because this is an exclusive retreat and places are limited!

The price: €1200 per person, cutoff date for reservations is end of February.

What’s included:

  • - Accommodation in shared rooms
  • - Instruction on swimming, yoga, breathwork and freediving
  • - Guided hike
  • - Kaiki hire with skipper
  • - Refreshments onboard the boat
  • - A farewell meal

What’s not included:

  • - Flights to Crete
  • - Transportation to Loutro (can be arranged by us on request)
  • - Meals
  • - Gear (contact us if you need to hire)

A non-refundable deposit of €150 is required by the 15th of February (to secure services). The remainder is required by the 10th of March.

Payment can be made via PayPal or Revolut.
