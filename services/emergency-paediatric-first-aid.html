This course is designed for those who are interested in being prepared to react in an emergency situation which involves children and infants.

The program is mainly addressed to the Child & Infant Basic Life Support skills (BLS) and optionally the use of the Automated External Defibrillator (A.E.D.) can be combined to the course.
