Services

Breathing / Relaxation Classes

Breathing and relaxation classes according to the Apnea Academy system. Beginner to advanced classes, incorporating stretches for breathing muscles and pranayama exercises. Specific exercises to counter stress and relieve tension.
Private or group sessions are both equally negotiable 🙂

Freediving Courses

Freediving courses based on the internationally acclaimed Apnea Academy system. All courses require a medical certificate. To pass all courses you need to have an adequate profile according to my judgement, pass the written test and perform the required time / depth. Available courses with curricula and performances are as follows:

Apnea Experience

This is an introductory course that can be done over two days. The minimum is 6 hours of training in the water (sea and/or pool), 2 hours of theory and 2 hours of breathing and relaxation techniques.

Indoor Apnea

This is geared for freedivers who are mostly focused on pool apnea or who don’t have access to the open water. It can also be done over the winter and combined with the Outdoor Apnea to get a Level 1 certificate.
– 4 theory lessons
– 4 relaxation & breathing technique lessons
– 8 hours of pool training minimum
To pass this course, you need to perform a static apnea of 1:45, a DYN of 30m and a DNF of 20m.

Outdoor Apnea

Required sessions for this course are:
– 4 theory lessons
– 4 relaxation & breathing technique lessons
– 2 open water training sessions
To pass this course, you need to do a CWT of -8m.

Level 1

Curriculum / performances:
– 5 theory lessons
– 4 breathing / relaxation / eustachian exercises lessons
– 12 hours of pool training minimum
– 4 hours of open water training minimum
To pass this course, you need to perform a static apnea of 1:45, a DYN of 30m, a DNF of 25m and a CWT of -8m.

Level 2

Curriculum / performances:
– 5 theory lessons
– 5 breathing / relaxation / eustachian exercises lessons
– 16 hours of pool training minimum
– 8 hours of open water training minimum
To pass this course, you need to perform a static apnea of 2:30, a DYN of 55m, a DNF of 35m, a DYN of 15m at -5m, a victim recovery from -8m and a CWT of -15m.

Level 3

Curriculum / performances:
– 4 theory lessons
– 6 breathing / relaxation / eustachian exercises lessons
– 20 hours of pool training minimum
– 12 hours of open water training minimum
To pass this course, you need to perform a static apnea of 3′, a DYN of 70m, a DNF of 55m, a DYN of 25m at -5m, a victim recovery from -15m and a CWT of -25m.
