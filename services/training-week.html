NEW FOR 2026!

You can now book a private one to one week with me!
Accommodation at my guest room is included.
We will cook together and share food costs for the week.
We will work in detail on technique, equalization and mental training.
We will analyse your dives daily and make notes for improvement on the following session.
You can also try spearfishing & skandalopetra diving.
And finally, I will show you around the island and its beautiful beaches.
This is the best way to get the most out of your time with me!

The cost is 1000 euros.

UPDATE:
Now included in the cost of the training week is an online equalization workshop prior to your arrival on the island at less than half the price.
As a SHARE Equalization instructor, I can help you learn or refine your equalization technique prior to your diving here, to make sure you can have the most fun here and not spend time struggling with equalization issues.

This is a full week of spending time together on the island. We will dive every day where the weather allows and I will take videos & photos of you and your dives. We will have one day off when we explore the island and its beaches, sites, restaurants etc together. We will snorkel to look for turtles and maybe also do some fishing if that's to your liking. Finally, we'll explore and dive around in caves and underwater swim-throughs, as well as a small shipwreck!

Additionally and upon further communication, we could hire a boat to reach spots that are inaccessible by car or foot or go on a sailboat tour of the island (depending on the weather this may be to the north and the shipwreck beach or the south and the Mizithres rocks). The cost of these activities is not included in the 500 euro fee for the week.

I will lend you weights for your belt at no extra cost, just make sure to bring your belt!

* The price does not change if sea conditions prohibit diving on one or more days.
