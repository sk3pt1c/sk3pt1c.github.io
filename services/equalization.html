As a SHARE Equalization instructor (you can find my profile here) and an Apnea Academy Equalization instructor (you can find my profile here) I am here to help you work on your equalization awareness and skills from the comfort of your home.

We can work together using one of the two available courses on the Share platform, Start or Deep.

The Start course is meant for students who are more beginners or need to get into better grips with Frenzel equalization, while the Deep is addressed to students who are comfortable with Frenzel and want to progress to equalizing deeper, using for example the Mouthfill technique.

Regardless of which course you choose, you will gain access to exercises on the platform (for a year).

We will then have three video calls (one per week) of around an hour so I can check your progress and guide you through the exercises so that you are performing them properly with the required level of control and awareness.

Having access to the SHARE method and with my assistance, you will be able to master equalization in virtually no time!

Once you complete the SHARE course, I will give you pool exercises from Apnea Academy to continue your equalization journey, increasing your comfort level gradually in a wet environment before heading out to sea.
