Agency Comparison

This is a comparison of different agencies standards when it comes to the minimum amount of sessions required to complete a course.
Some agencies have different names or more levels, I have tried to use Levels 1 through 3 for all, where there are differences I have focused on the requirements to create equivalence.
I apologize if I have made any mistakes, please contact me to correct them.
