Welcome dear visitor, my name is Ioannis Aliazis and I am (among other things) a freediving instructor with the Apnea Academy school.

Free flow is my first foray into the world as an instructor of all things breath and relaxation.

My goal is to meet people and help them understand the importance of knowing how to breathe and relax and how this applies in freediving as well as in every day life.

Free flow stands for “freedom through flow experiences”, meaning trying to achieve mental freedom and calm by participating in activities that induce a feeling of “flow”.

So, I invite you to join me on this journey of self discovery through learning to breathe, relax and freedive.

Please explore the website and email me with any questions.

You can also find me on Facebook and Instagram.
