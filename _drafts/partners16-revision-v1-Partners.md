**[Apnea Academy](https://apnea.academy/en/home/)&nbsp;School for the Instruction and Research of Freediving**

The Apnea Academy is by far the best school right now for freediving, its courses are the longest and more involved and its focus is breathing and relaxation first and foremost, what constitutes the core of freediving and any pleasurable flow experience at large.&nbsp;  
Their instructors course is the most difficult and is run by Umberto Pelizzari himself alongside a team of experts (each in their own field) and instructors, which is why I chose it.  
[Here](https://apnea.academy/en/instructors/?formregione=Greece&formsurname=&submit=Search#)&nbsp;you can see my profile page on the AA website’s list of instructors for Greece.<figure class="wp-block-image">

<img src="https://free-flow.gr/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/g-sea-logo.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-167" /> </figure> 

G & Sea is an excellent wetsuit manufacturer based in Athens, Greece. I have the privilege and the honour to be part of their operation since the beginning and am proud that we only have happy customers so far. Gilda has an experience of over 15 years in making wetsuits and is constantly trying to improve her designs and processes. We work exclusively with Yamamoto materials and make orders several times within each year to ensure that customers get as fresh neoprene as possible.  
You can find G & Sea on the&nbsp;[website](https://www.g-and-sea.com/)&nbsp;and&nbsp;[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/gandsea/).<figure class="wp-block-image">

<img src="https://free-flow.gr/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/onebreath-logo.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-168" /> </figure> 

One Breath is the freediving school of my friend, coach and mentor Dimitris Koumoulos, based in Athens, Greece. He is the only instructor I would recommend to anyone wanting to do a course in Athens. Dimitris is a great guy, an experienced instructor and trainer and a former competitive swimmer, so you will definitely be in good hands.  
He also organises competitions, the most famous of which is the&nbsp;[Authentic Big Blue](https://www.facebook.com/authenticbigblue/), which is run annually on the island of Amorgos, where scenes of the infamous Big Blue movie were shot.  
You can find One Breath on the&nbsp;[website](https://www.onebreath.gr/)&nbsp;and&nbsp;[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/onebreath.gr/).
