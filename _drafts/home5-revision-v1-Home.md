Welcome dear visitor, my name is Ioannis Aliazis and I am a freediving instructor with the Apnea Academy school.

Free flow is my school as an instructor of all things breath and relaxation.

My goal is to meet adults as well as children and help them understand the importance of knowing how to breathe and relax and how this applies in freediving as well as in every day life.

Free flow stands for “freedom through flow experiences”, meaning trying to achieve mental freedom and calm by participating in activities that induce a feeling of “flow”.

So, I invite you to join me on this journey of self discovery through learning to breathe, relax and freedive.
